Document Management System (DMS)

Most organisations manage documents across email threads, shared drives, and messaging apps — leading to version chaos, lost files, and compliance gaps. We design SharePoint-based DMS solutions that bring order, access control, and full searchability to your document landscape.

  • ✓Structured metadata taxonomy and document classification
  • ✓Role-based access control and permission inheritance
  • ✓Version history, check-in/check-out, and full audit trails
  • ✓Automated retention policies and compliance tagging
  • ✓Full-text search across 10,000+ documents in seconds
  • ✓AI-powered document summarisation via Copilot integration
Typical Outcome
Document retrieval reduced from 15 minutes to under 30 seconds. Zero misplaced files after go-live.
